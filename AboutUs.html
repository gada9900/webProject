About us

Digital art is an artistic work or practice that uses digital technology as part of the creative or presentation process. More generally the term digital artist is used to describe an artist who makes use of digital technologies in the production of art. Our website displays various Artists with their Artwork, also it shows types of art exhibition , set multiple competitions and suggests courses for our lovely visitors.
